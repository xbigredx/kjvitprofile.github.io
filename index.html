My Project Idea

Overview

The idea behind my project is quite simple. Given the amount of demand for building and construction within the area I live in. Things are starting to get complicated and reading house plans can be difficult at the best of times. My proposal is an augmented reality system that allows the builder to be able to see the house plans on the building site in real time. This system will allow the contractors to see exactly where specific elements of the house will be located, elements such as light fixtures, PowerPoints, internal walls etc.

Motivation

The motivation behind this idea is since construction in the Albury/Wodonga area since the pandemic has boomed, with enquiries with some builders up 300% and sales up between 30 to 40% (Howie, 2021.) With this surge in demand now, the industry is very busy, and time is a very precious thing for them. One of the more time-consuming parts of building is ready a set of house plans and being able to interpret those plans so that the house is built according to those plans. By using this system and being able to set this plan in the real time environment what the house will look like, this will save a significant amount of time by not having to read and interpret house plans.

What is the Product?

For most house builds that occur these days, the work is generally carried out by subcontractors who will work on various building sites throughout the week. This product will allow them to seamlessly go from job to job and have minimal delays in having to pull out the plans and read them. With the rise in computer aided design or CAD for short. These files will allow this product to work well as this will eliminate the need to digitize any hand drawn house plans.

After the concrete slab has been poured for the house and has cured enough to be safe to work on. The contractor can place four sensors in the corners of the house slab. These will connect to a device, tablet or phone via Bluetooth and these sensors will act as positional guidance for your device. This will ensure that when you are looking at a wall the sensors will ensure that you are looking at the correct wall based on your position. Then on your device through your camera you will be able to see in front of you the house layout in real time with a graphically altered image of what you see on your camera. This graphically altered image will show you the layout of the house such as where the internal walls of the house will be located and will also provide measurements for the wall length etc.

One other feature of this product will be allowing you to set what stage of the build the project is up to so that the information that is being shown to you is relevant to what step of the project you are up to. For example, it is irrelevant to be detailing where power outlets are to be located when the frame of the house is being erected.

Development

Regarding the development of this product, there will need to be some considerations when it comes down to what technology is required and what skills are needed to be able to develop this product. The first being that the technology needed will be an app that can run on both phones and tablets and be able to take data from a computer. The other technology that is required are the sensors that will provide the tracking of the device and where it is situated in the house at the time.

Skills in writing codes in different languages will be the first critical skill that is required as there will be a need to write the code for an app that will work across multiple different platforms. There will also be a need for an electrical engineer to develop the sensors required for this product to work. Unless there is a viable option to be able to buy a sensor.

Benefits

The overall benefit that this product will bring comes down to the amount of time that is saved. This comes down to the amount of time a contractor will save by not having to read the plans in detail. By doing this as well, this will result in less mistakes being made which will save even more time as the contractor will not have to return to the site to fix these errors. Overall, the time that is saved can reduce the overall costs of building a house which is especially important with the rising costs of house currently.
